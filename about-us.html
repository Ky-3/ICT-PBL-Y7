About The Waterguards

We are a group of Year 7 students dedicated to protecting Yangon's water security through this website and our project model.

Our Aim

To raise community awareness about the growing water pollution in Yangon's waterways, such as the Nga Moe Yeik Creek, and inspire residents to adopt cleaner daily habits.

Our Mission

Using our research, web platform, and interactive scale models to prove that community can successfully restore local water quality for a healthier 2050.
